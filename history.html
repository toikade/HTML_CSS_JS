Our Story.

Well our history.. where should i start.. You won't believe me if I told you. Ok! I'll try. Go outside and look at the Sun. If you saw it, well.. we're older than the sun you saw. So trust us.

It all started like a joke when I was very hungry and had to eat, so I began searching for food in this deserted land until i stumbled upon this deliciosus steakus,(well.. I think that's the scientific name), it was one of a kind and I think it should have been the only one perhaps still living. To cut the long story short, the steakus did not particularly prove easy to capture using conventional ways at that time so i had to resort to science to help me out. Since that day I vowed to discover tools to help me out on this and all those tools have been used by others in their daily tasks. Although till this day I have not yet captured the steakus, my genius team and I and always on the watch for new discoveries.
